Classpublic class AudioDescriptor
InheritanceAudioDescriptor Object

An AudioDescriptor describes the characteristics of an audio stream in terms of number of channels and sample rate. Every IAudioSource instance is associated with a AudioDescriptor that indicates what kind of audio data is available from it.
