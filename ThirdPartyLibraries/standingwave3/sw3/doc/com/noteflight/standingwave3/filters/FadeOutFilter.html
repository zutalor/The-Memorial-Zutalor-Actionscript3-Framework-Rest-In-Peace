Packagecom.noteflight.standingwave3.filters
Classpublic class FadeOutFilter
InheritanceFadeOutFilter AbstractFilter Object

FadeOutFilter passes the signal unchanged until the fade is reached, and then fades it out with the supplied envelope. Many times a fade can be used in place of an Envelope + AmpFilter and will be much more efficient, since it only calculates during the fading part.
